# Smart Household Advisor

## Context and Motivation
Managing household expenses and energy consumption is increasingly a significant challenge, both economically and environmentally. Rising energy costs and the complexity of tariffs make it difficult to clearly understand where the major expenses lie and which behaviors significantly impact the family budget.  

Traditional tools do not provide an integrated view of both expenses and energy usage, nor do they allow immediate assessment of the impact of potential future decisions. This need has led to the development of **Smart Household Advisor**, a system capable of analyzing historical data and supporting users in the conscious management of their household resources.

## System Objectives
The project aims to create a decision support platform for the integrated analysis of household expenses and energy consumption. The system allows processing of historical data categorized by type, identifying trends, and providing summary reports through dedicated APIs.  

Additionally, it integrates "what-if" simulation functionalities to estimate potential savings from specific operational choices. From a design perspective, the objectives include applying principles of modularity, separation of concerns, and security mechanisms based on JWT (JSON Web Token) authentication and role-based access control (RBAC).
